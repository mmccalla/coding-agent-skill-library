# Business Architecture Skills Manifest

Use these skills for capabilities, value streams, processes, operating models, maturity, organisation and strategy-to-execution traceability.

| Skill | Use when |
|---|---|
| `business-capability-modelling` | Defining stable business abilities and capability maps. |
| `value-stream-modelling` | Mapping trigger-to-outcome value creation. |
| `process-modelling` | Modelling operational steps, decisions, hand-offs and controls. |
| `operating-model-design` | Designing people, process, technology, data, governance, funding and delivery alignment. |
| `strategy-to-execution-traceability` | Linking objectives, outcomes, capabilities, value streams, initiatives and metrics. |
| `capability-maturity-assessment` | Assessing current/target maturity, gaps and roadmap priorities. |
| `business-information-concept-modelling` | Deriving business information concepts from business architecture artefacts. |
| `organisation-and-role-design` | Defining roles, decision rights, accountabilities and team boundaries. |
